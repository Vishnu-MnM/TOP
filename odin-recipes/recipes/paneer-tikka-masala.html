Paneer Tikka Masala

Hello! Welcome to my recipe (well, technically not mine) of paneer tikka masala.

This paneer tikka masala made with cubes of paneer cheese in a spicy creamy curry sauce is easy to make. This vegetarian dish goes well with naan or basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 pound of paneer, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 1 medium bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 medium jalapeño peppers, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 pint half-and-half
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add paneer cubes; cook and stir until golden, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add onion, bell pepper, jalapeños, ground cashews, garlic paste, ginger paste, cayenne pepper, cumin, coriander, and garam masala; cook and stir until well combined and fragrant, about 1 minute.

  4. Mix tomato sauce, half-and-half, and salt into paneer mixture; simmer until thickened, about 30 minutes.
